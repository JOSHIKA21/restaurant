Our Journey

Here we wish to share a fruitful history of Haribhavanam Hotels. Our humble starting was the result of innovative idea of Mr. Raju who was in charge of Haribhavanam lodge situated at 4th street, Gandhipuram, Coimbatore. As all inmates of the lodge were looking for some place to have a homely food.



Our founder took the initiative to provide homely food to the inmates of Haribhavanam lodge which gradually developed in to Haribhavanam Hotels or commonly known as Raju mess among the Non Vegetarian food lovers.
